Remy's Food Truck

Remy’s Food truck is a regional fast food maker located in a metropolitan area. Remy’s Food truck strives to deliver healthy food quickly at affordable price. They offer a wide spectrum of competitive pricing. We have created new app to help people order healthy and affordable food easily and quickly.

As a UX Designer, my responsibility was to conduct user research, create paper and digital wireframes for app as well as low and high-fidelity prototypes, conduct usability studies to determine further pain points ans issues, accomodate accessibility features and iterating on designs.
